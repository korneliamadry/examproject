Poisonous plants in a "cat's house"

At home, not all cats instincts work properly. Sometimes domestic cats try to eat things that may harm them. Cat owners should protect cats from poisonous substances, for example found in plants in home or garden.

How to recognize that a cat has been poisoned?

Plant toxins often do not give symptoms right away. Cats feel good until suddenly his condition get worse and the veterinary research shows irreversibly damaged kidneys and liver, burns of the digestive system. Possible symptoms after poisoning: vomiting, convulsions, diarrhea, lack of appetite, brigher gums and tongue, muscle relaxation, confusion, coma, increased salivation, difficulty swallowing.
